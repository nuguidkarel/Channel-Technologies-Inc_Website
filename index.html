SIGNAL 02 — ABOUT US

Vision, evolution, and
purpose‑driven innovation.

The Channel Group of Companies began its journey in 1993, founded by Manuel S. Bagatsing (MSB) — a non‑technical entrepreneur with a sharp eye for pain points in the banking sector. His focus on cost‑effective banking‑system hardware maintenance built the group's early reputation as a trusted industry partner.

As digital transformation accelerated in the early 2000s, MSB moved the group toward a more software‑driven landscape. In 2003, Channel Technologies launched its software and professional services arm, Channel Solutions Inc., to meet growing demand for agile solutions across enterprise resource planning, loan management, and digital transformation — and it quickly became a leading provider of Software Lifecycle Management (SLM) for banking, retail, cooperatives, and beyond.

The group's reach didn't stop there. MSB expanded into subsidiaries covering e‑loading services, cable services, petrol equipment, farming, and more — each built to fill gaps in underserved sectors with sustainable, forward‑thinking solutions.

Today, Channel Technologies Inc. and Channel Solutions Inc. stand as the pillars of a larger, dynamic group serving a broad range of industries — still guided by MSB's core mission: purposeful innovation and smart technology that improves businesses and lives.

SIGNAL 04 — PRODUCTS & SOLUTIONS

Cutting‑edge solutions for
secure, efficient transactions.

From cash automation to encryption and customer intelligence, our product line equips banks, retailers, and enterprises with future‑ready, secure technology.

ATM

Automated Teller Machines that eliminate the need for dedicated cash handling staff — evolved from pure dispensing into accept, recycle, and other self‑service functions.

Cash Recycling Machines

Intelligent cash‑handling that automates deposits and withdrawals, cutting cash‑handling costs and improving operational efficiency.

Payment Gateway

Orchestrates fund movements within the organization and across financial‑services providers via API, supporting BancNet, InstaPay, PesoNet, and other local network messaging.

mPOS & EFTPOS

An agency‑banking platform enabling electronic funds transfers via debit or credit cards, plus cardless transactions through QR codes.

QR Code Dispensing

Secure, contactless cash withdrawal technology using QR codes — built for customer convenience and fraud prevention.

EFT Switch

The Millennium Hub — a Windows Server‑based ATM switch system that gives banks 24‑hour banking across ATM, Kiosk, and EFTPOS channels.

Hardware Security Modules

The industry‑standard encryption component used by major banks worldwide. CTI provides roughly 80% of the HSMs in the Philippines, largely handling PIN encryption for bank customers.

Customer Information System

A centralized platform integrating customer data across banking channels, enabling personalized service and better decision‑making.

Customer Loyalty Program

Rewards customers with tailored services, including VIP handling, and enables customer profiling so services can be personalized.
